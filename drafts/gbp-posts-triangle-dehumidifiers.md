One of the most common questions we get: do you need full crawl space encapsulation, or will a dehumidifier handle it?

The honest answer is — it depends on what's actually going on down there.

A dehumidifier works in both vented and encapsulated crawl spaces. Encapsulation is the right call when there's visible mold, wood rot, or water intrusion that needs to be solved at the source first.

We broke down the differences, the costs ($3,000–$5,000 for a dehu, $7,500–$15,000 for full encapsulation + dehu), and the decision framework we use on every inspection.
