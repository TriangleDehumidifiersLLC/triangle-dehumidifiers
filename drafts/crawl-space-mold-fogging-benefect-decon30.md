## Full-Length Version (for website/blog use):

Mold in your crawl space is more than a cosmetic problem. It compromises your home's air quality, weakens wood framing, and creates musty odors that rise through the floor into your living space. At Triangle Dehumidifiers, we treat crawl space mold using Benefect Decon 30 — a hospital-grade, EPA-registered botanical disinfectant trusted by restoration professionals nationwide.

### Why We Use Benefect Decon 30

Not all mold treatments are created equal. Many conventional disinfectants rely on harsh synthetic chemicals that leave behind toxic residues — a real concern in the confined, poorly ventilated environment of a crawl space. Benefect Decon 30 takes a different approach.

Its active ingredient is Thymol, derived from thyme oil. Despite being plant-based, Decon 30 is no lightweight. It kills over 99.99% of bacteria, mold, fungal spores, viruses, and odor-causing microorganisms. The EPA has approved it to eliminate Trichophyton mentagrophytes — the representative organism for the entire mold, fungi, and yeast category and one of the hardest to kill. It is also effective against Aspergillus niger, a common crawl space mold species, and Candida albicans.

What sets Decon 30 apart from standard Benefect disinfectant is its effectiveness on porous surfaces. Wood floor joists, subfloor sheathing, and rim boards in your crawl space are all porous materials where mold takes hold. Third-party studies show Decon 30 is very powerful even on these porous surfaces — not just hard, non-porous ones.

Other key advantages:

- **30-second kill time** on hard surfaces — most disinfectants require 10 minutes of wet contact time, which is nearly impossible to maintain on vertical or overhead surfaces in a crawl space
- **No rinsing required** — leaves no harmful residue on treated surfaces
- **Safe for food-contact surfaces** — no synthetic chemicals, no harsh fumes
- **No PPE required** for handling — safe for both the technician and your family
- **EPA-registered, USDA BioPreferred certified, and UL EcoLogo certified**
- **Eliminates odors** caused by mold, bacteria, and other microorganisms on both porous and non-porous surfaces

### How Our Mold Fogging Process Works

We apply Benefect Decon 30 using ULV (ultra-low volume) fogging equipment that produces an extremely fine mist — particles smaller than mold spores themselves. This allows the treatment to reach every crack, crevice, joist bay, and surface throughout your crawl space, including areas that would be impossible to reach with a hand sprayer alone.

Our process follows a thorough, multi-step approach:

1. **Inspection and moisture source identification.** Mold does not grow without moisture. Before we treat a single surface, we identify what is feeding the mold — standing water, ground vapor, condensation on pipes, or humid outside air entering through open vents.

2. **HEPA vacuuming of affected surfaces.** We remove loose mold spores and debris from floor joists, subfloor, sill plates, and foundation walls using HEPA-filtered equipment. This is the first half of the "HEPA sandwich" approach recommended by remediation professionals — even dead mold spores can release mycotoxins, so physical removal matters.

3. **Benefect Decon 30 fogging application.** The ULV fogger disperses Decon 30 as a fine mist that fills the entire crawl space, coating every surface including porous wood framing where mold takes hold. We ensure treated surfaces remain wet for the required contact time to achieve full disinfection.

4. **Second HEPA vacuuming pass.** After the treatment has dried, we perform a second HEPA vacuum pass to remove dead mold spores and any remaining particulate. This completes the HEPA sandwich — remove, disinfect, remove.

5. **Moisture control recommendations.** Killing existing mold is only half the job. Without addressing the moisture source, mold will return. We provide a clear plan for long-term prevention — whether that means installing a crawl space dehumidifier, encapsulating the crawl space with a sealed vapor barrier, or both.

### What Mold Fogging Does and Does Not Do

We believe in being straightforward with our customers. Mold fogging with Benefect Decon 30 is highly effective at killing active mold, fungal spores, bacteria, and the microorganisms that cause musty crawl space odors. It reaches surfaces and cavities that hand-spraying cannot.

However, fogging alone is not a complete solution. The EPA and industry standards (IICRC S520) are clear: effective mold remediation requires identifying and correcting the moisture source, physically removing contaminated materials when necessary, disinfecting remaining surfaces, and verifying the work. Fogging is a powerful tool within that process — not a substitute for it.

That is why we pair our mold treatment with moisture management solutions. A crawl space dehumidifier maintains relative humidity below 60%, eliminating the conditions mold needs to grow. Encapsulation seals out ground moisture and humid exterior air permanently. Together with professional mold fogging, these services provide both an immediate solution and long-term protection.

For a typical residential crawl space in the Raleigh, NC area, this means thorough coverage of all wood surfaces, foundation walls, and exposed areas in a single treatment session.
